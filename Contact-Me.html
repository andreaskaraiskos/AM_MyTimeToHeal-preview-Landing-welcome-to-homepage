The Practice is easily accessible from most Central and Greater London locations either via tube, rail or road. 

Parking

There is Free On-Street Parking

By Car

The practice is conveniently located in the HA0 vicinity on the borders of the neighbouring Ealing and Harrow boroughs not far from The Clementine Churchill, Northwick Park and St. Mark's Hospitals

It is approx. a 10-15 minute drive from the North Circular Road, Alperton and the A40 Western Avenue, Greenford. 

Further Details

To plan your journey from your location, there are useful sites to assist you including:
Google Maps, AA Route Planner, RAC Route Planner, Green Flag Route Planner, City Mapper

Public Transport

The area is well serviced by public transport. Below is some information to assist you in planning your journey.

Bus Routes

Bus Routes 483, 182, N18, 245, H17 and 92 all bring you within 3 - 15 minute walking distance from the practice.

Underground Routes

Harrow-on-the-Hill (Metropolitan Line), Sudbury Town Station (Piccadilly Line), Sudbury Hill Station (Piccadilly Line), Sudbury Hill Harrow (Chiltern Overground), Sudbury & Harrow Road (Chiltern Overground), South Kenton Station (Bakerloo Line / National Rail, Silverlink), North Wembley Station (Bakerloo Line / National Rail, Silverlink. Wembley Park (Jubilee Line / Metropolitan Line)

All offer easy access with short walking distances or by bus from outside the stations.
To plan your journey from your personal location, visit tfl.gov.uk

More detailed

Information on how to get to the practice will be emailed to you once your consultation appointment is booked.
If you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Accessibility

The practice entrance is accessible via a short pathway. There are some steps outside the main entrance. Unfortunately, there is no wheelchair access.

There is no waiting area at the practice, so please arrive promptly at your appointment time.

Visits to the practice are strictly by appointment only
